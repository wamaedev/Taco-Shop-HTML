Taco Trivia

When did Tacos first appear in the USA?

Jeffrey M. Pilcher, taco historian and professor of history at the University of Minnesota, says the earliest mention of Tacos in the USA are in a newspaper from 1905. (Source: Smithsonian Magazine)
